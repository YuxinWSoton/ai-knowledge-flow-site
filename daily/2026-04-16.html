  • 链接：https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.07062v1
  • 来源：arxiv
  • 摘要：We classify $n\times n$-matrix-valued continuous commutativity and spectrum preservers defined on spaces of (a) normal, (b) semisimple and (c) arbitrary $n\times n$ matrices with spectra contained in sufficiently connected subsets $\mathcal{X}\subseteq \mathbb{C}$, generalizing a number of results due to Šemrl, Gogić, Tomašević and the author among others. In case (a) these are always conjugations or transpose conjugations, while in cases (b) and (c) qualitatively distinct possibilities arise depending on the local regularity of the complex-conjugation map close to coincident-eigenvalue loci of $\mathcal{X}^n$.

Eigenvalue collision，即矩阵共轭特征值的现象，对矩阵谱的影响至关重要。局部共轭映射的正则性决定了保结构的可能性，而共轭和转置共轭总是保持正规矩阵的性质。对于半单和任意矩阵，质上不同的可能性随之出现，这些可能性受到共轭特征值区域的影响。因此，充分连通的子集在复数域中被考虑用于矩阵谱的研究。这种分类不仅推广了该领域的先前结果，还揭示了复杂共轭映射的局部正则性对于理解保结构的重要性。此外，在特定条件下，可以出现不同于共轭的奇异保结构，这些奇异保结构在理解矩阵保结构方面提供了新的视角。

  • 链接：https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14735v1
  • 来源：arxiv
  • 摘要：The aim of this paper is to introduce the notion of (noncommutative) transposed Poisson conformal algebras, which serve as the conformal analogues of transposed Poisson algebras and admit a rich class of identities. We show that the tensor product of two transposed Poisson conformal algebras is also a transposed Poisson conformal algebra. Moreover, we establish a close relationship between transposed Poisson conformal algebras and Hom-Lie conformal algebras, and give the compatibility conditions between a Poisson conformal algebra and a transposed Poisson conformal algebra. In addition, we provide several constructions of transposed Poisson conformal algebras arising from related algebraic structures. Finally, a complete classification of compatible noncommutative transposed Poisson conformal algebraic structures over a class of Lie conformal algebras W(a, b) is given.
